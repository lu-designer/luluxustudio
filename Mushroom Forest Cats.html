Forest Mushroom Cats

Once upon a time, two curious kitties ventured into a mystical forest and disappeared for days and weeks. Eventually, they were discovered frolicking among whimsically shaped mushrooms. Painting this scene was an absolute joy! It was a process of imagining myself playing with these kitties in a colourful mushroom wonderland!
